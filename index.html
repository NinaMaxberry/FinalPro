ABOUT US

The Children's Rainbow Project is a continuation of a non-profit youth foundation with its origin in Manassas Park, Virginia in 2005. YES YOU CAN! YOUNG LADY! YOUNG MAN! was a nonprofit foundation established by its founder Hosea ("Hatcat") Mills. The foundation was dedicated to mentoring youth from the ages of 7 to 17.

Shortly after establishing the foundation, Mr. Mills selected Nina Maxberry as the foundations Executive Director. Together they defined the foundations mission as not specific to race or religion, but instead the opportunity to build positive self-confidence, job skills and literacy in the younger generation overall.
Programs included a Life Cycle program that introduced life skills in financial planning, career building, personal development, along with a community service program for juveniles with legal obligations, and counseling programs for substance abuse, incarceration and gang affiliation.

We invite you to visit our Program page and discover the area you would like to receive more information. Of course, your interest in helping is always appreciated.
